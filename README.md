# Free Printable Activity Sheets for Kids

Ready-to-print A4 activity sheets for kids in 9 languages — car bingo, summer bingo, nature bingo, beach bingo, rebus puzzles, treasure hunts and school planners. Made by [Zenframe](https://www.zenframe.no/?utm_source=github&utm_medium=repo&utm_campaign=free-printables), the family planning app from Bergen, Norway.

**How to use:** pick your language below, download the PDF and print it at home. Each sheet here is the age 7–9 edition — every activity also comes in 4–6 and 10–12 editions plus themed variants (dinosaur, pirate, space, princess…), all free on zenframe.no via the links in the tables.

## About

These sheets are generated by the activity workshop on [zenframe.no/aktiviteter](https://www.zenframe.no/aktiviteter?utm_source=github&utm_medium=repo&utm_campaign=free-printables) — an interactive generator that builds unique boards per child (so siblings don't get identical sheets). This repo is synced from the same pipeline.
